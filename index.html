The ERS Standard — Every Interaction

A tow driver responding to a breakdown at midnight doesn't open with their employee number. They open with "You okay? Let's get you home." Security & life safety operates at the same urgency. A panel beeping at 3 AM is not a tech ticket — it's someone's anxiety about their family. Lead with the person. Solve second.

💬 The S·B·E Framework — Drill These Until Automatic

🟢 Starters — Open the Door

"You did the right thing calling us." / "Let's take care of this together right now." / "You're in the right place."

🟡 Betweeners — Hold the Trust

"I want to get this exactly right for you." / "I'm not going anywhere." / "That's a completely fair concern — let me address it directly."

🔴 Enders — Seal It

"Anything else before I let you go?" / "You have my word on that." / "Thank you for trusting us with your home."

⚡ Non-Negotiables

Use their name. Validate before solving. Never interrupt. Translate every term. Match urgency, not frustration.
